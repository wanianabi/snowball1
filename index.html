8trapy - A watch, an application, an assistant

8trapy is a watch that is designed for people with a drug addiction. The watch is connected to an application that keeps track of your heart rate, sleep cycle, reactivity and body temperature. Your user information will be available for an assistant that will guide you and keep track of your data. The assistant will help you set some personal goals and will lead you through your journey.
